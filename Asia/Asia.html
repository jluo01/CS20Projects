Shanghai
Vibrant, Cosmopolitan, Bright.
Shanghai is always a must-stop for both local and international travelers. No matter what you are feeling for, Shanghai has something to offer: spiritual temples, inspiring museums, modern architecture, classical parks, and tea houses.

Charming, Bustling, Wondrous.
Tokyo hosts myriad choices of sight-watching, shopping, and entertainment for its visitors. Ancient temples nestled among modern skyscrapers, beautiful cherry blossoms in the late spring, and vibrant night scenes in Tokyo's central district.
Bangkok
Inspiring, Adventurous, Noble.
Holding its reputation as the "City of Angels," Bangkok thrives on its enriching culture and buzzing city life. Whether it's magnificent temples, historic markets, or modern skyscrapers, Bangkok has her story to share.
