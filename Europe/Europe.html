Europe

Unbelievable variety.

Murren:
Natural, Majestic, Free.
After a short cable car ride, a charming village in the Swiss alps awaits you. Whether enjoying a hike in alps, basking in the breathtaking scenery, or grabbing a bite in the local restaurants, there's lots to do and see.
Zurich:
Active, welcoming, luxurious.
One of the best kept treasures in Europe. Zurich has world class music shopping, and people. Swim in the unbelievably clear Lake Zurich, drink pristine water from any of the ornate public fountains, and consume all of the sights and history around you.
Lucerne
Historic, beautiful, idylic.
Lucerne brings artists and music lovers from around the world to its namesake music festival. Walk down the winding cobblestone roads, grab a gelato from a street vendor, and see what else this city has to offer!
